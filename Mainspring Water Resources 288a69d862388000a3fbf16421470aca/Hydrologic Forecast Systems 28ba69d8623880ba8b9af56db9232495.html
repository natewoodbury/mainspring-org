U.S. Bureau of Reclamation (USBR), Eastern Colorado Area Office

(2013)

Directed the development of tools to assist in automated acquisition, processing, and display of precipitation, streamflow, and reservoir data for both forecast system operation and system management.

El Cajon Hydrometeorologic Data Collection and Forecast System Development

Comisión Nacional del Agua (CONAGUA)/NWS

(2002-2003)

Project Manager

Managed the development of a hydrologic forecast system for the Río Santiago in Central Mexico to provide flood warning for the construction of the El Cajon Hydroelectric project. The project involved data collection, analysis, model calibration, and real-time forecast system implementation.

Assisted in the development and verification of discharge rating curves for a network of automated hydrometeorological stations. Developed training material and provided training to Mexican engineers in forecast system operation, including procedures for updating rating curves.
